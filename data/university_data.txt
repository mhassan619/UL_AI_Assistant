[SECTION: STUDENT LIFE, RULES, & EXAMINATION GUIDELINES]
- Attendance Policy: Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each registered course to be eligible to sit in the final term examinations. Falling below 75% results in an automatic course drop or 'F' grade due to short attendance.
- Grading System Scale: University follows the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) aligned with HEC guidelines. The maximum possible CGPA is 4.00. The passing marks threshold for any individual subject course is strictly 50%.
- Examination Schedule: Midterm examinations are held in the 8th week of the ongoing semester. Final term examinations take place in the 16th week. 
- Campus Discipline: Ragging, smoking, political profiling, and union strikes are strictly banned inside university territory. Wearing and carrying a valid Student ID Card at all times is mandatory for entry.


[SECTION: HOSTEL, ACCOMMODATION, & TRANSPORTATION SYSTEMS]
- On-Campus Hostel Accommodation: Available for both male and female students at the Main Campus. Seats are allocated strictly on a distance and merit basis. Hostel allocation forms can be collected from the Student Service Center.
- Hostel Fees: Annual hostel dues are 18,500 PKR (excluding mess charges). Mess charges are calculated dynamically on a monthly consumption basis.
- University Transport Fleet: Buses operate on designated routes connecting Layyah City, Chowk Azam, Fatehpur, and Karor Lal Esan directly to the university campuses. Transport card fee is 3,500 PKR per semester.


[SECTION: SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID PROGRAMS]
- HEC Need-Based Scholarships: Fully funds tuition fees and provides a monthly stipend for deserving, financially challenged students. Applications open in October each academic year.
- Punjab Educational Endowment Fund (PEEF): Available for students holding a Punjab domicile with at least 60% marks in intermediate.
- Merit Scholarships: Awarded automatically to the top 3 position holders of each department every semester based on their GPA results, providing a 50% to 100% tuition fee waiver.


[SECTION: CAMPUS FACILITIES & EVENTS LIFECYCLE]
- Central Library: Positioned on the Second Floor of the Main Block. Operational hours are 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Offers digital library terminals with IEEE, ACM, and HEC digital repository access.
- Library Card Process: Students must submit 2 passport-sized photographs and their university admission fee challan copy to the Library Registrar Office to secure a book issuance card.
- Main Sports Gala 2026: Scheduled to launch on November 12, 2026. Features official inter-departmental tournaments in Cricket, Football, Badminton, Table Tennis, and Athletics.
